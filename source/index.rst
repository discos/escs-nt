DISCOS NOTO 0.2 CONFIGURATION AND SETUP
=======================================
Welcome to the documentation of DISCOS setup for NOTO radiotelescope.
DISCOS is the *Development of italian Single-Disch COntrol System* which permits the
radiotelescope to be used by astronomers in single dish observations. The system
is the analogous of Nuraghe, installed at SRT, and ESCS installed at Medicina.
